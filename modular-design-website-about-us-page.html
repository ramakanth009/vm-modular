Our Story

Started by Two Visionaries

In a world where urban living often means compromising on space, two visionaries saw an opportunity to change the paradigm. It happened just as Venu Gopal Reddy was furnishing his new home in the city. On the other hand, Mr. Raghu Ram, a seasoned industry professional, was well-versed in the secrets of crafting homes and how to make them feel bigger, more functional, and genuinely personal.

Their chance meeting led to a powerful collaboration — a solution that would make Indian homes smarter, more functional, and aesthetically pleasing. Thus VM Modular was born, using precision engineering and German technology to their advantage.
